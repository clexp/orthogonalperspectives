I love maths. It's really great. There's always only two outcomes, you're either right or you're wrong. It's so much better than everything else in life. It all has unknown and probabalistic outcomes.

I know right now in the Sun will rise, there will be food on the table. I don't like uncertainty of that vague remark? Nobody likes uncertainty telling someone that they're definitely gonna die in the next three months, till even thank me, telling me that I don't know whether they're going to have a shortened life or not. That provides a lot of anxiety. But maths is great. Maths is really good. You are either right, or you are wrong, there's no uncertainty.
